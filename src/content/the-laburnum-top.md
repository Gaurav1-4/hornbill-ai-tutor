# The Laburnum Top

📝 **Short Summary:** The Laburnum tree is silent and still in autumn until a Goldfinch arrives. The bird's arrival brings the tree to life with the chirping of her chicks. When she flies away, the tree becomes silent and empty again.

1. **Important Points:** The bird is the "engine of her family."
2. **Literary Devices:** *Simile* ("sleek as a lizard"), *Metaphor* ("engine of her family").
3. **Exam Questions:** *How does the tree transform when the bird arrives? (3 Marks)*
4. **Answer Writing Tips:** Contrast the *death-like silence* at the beginning/end with the *thrills and tremors* in the middle.
