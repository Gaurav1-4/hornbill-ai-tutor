# Silk Road

📝 **Short Summary:** The author travels from Ravu to Mount Kailash to perform the *kora*. The journey is physically grueling due to high altitude and harsh terrain. He meets Norbu, a Tibetan working in Beijing, and they decide to do the *kora* together.

1. **Simple Explanation:** The author takes a tough road trip across Tibet. He suffers from a severe cold and altitude sickness. The town of Darchen is dusty and depressing until he meets Norbu, which brings him hope and companionship.
2. **Important Points:** 
   - The Tibetan mastiffs that chase their car.
   - The medical treatment given by the Tibetan doctor (Tibetan traditional medicine).
3. **Exam Questions:** *Describe the author's physical condition in Darchen. (3 Marks)*
4. **Answer Writing Tips:** Mention the *altitude sickness*, *sleeplessness*, and the feeling of isolation before meeting Norbu.
