# Father to Son

📝 **Short Summary:** A father laments that despite living in the same house for years, he doesn't understand his son. He wants his son, who has grown distant, to return home so they can rebuild their relationship and forgive each other.

1. **Important Points:** The father is willing to forgive the son just to have him back ("prodigal son" reference).
2. **Literary Devices:** *Metaphor* ("The seed I spent or sown it where the land is his"), *Alliteration* ("silence surrounds us").
3. **Exam Questions:** *Why is the father unhappy? (3 Marks)*
4. **Answer Writing Tips:** Emphasize the *communication gap* and the father's *desperate longing* for reconciliation.
