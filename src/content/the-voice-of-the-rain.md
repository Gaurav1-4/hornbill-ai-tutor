# The Voice of the Rain

📝 **Short Summary:** The poet asks the falling rain, "Who are you?" The rain replies that it is the "Poem of Earth." It describes its water cycle (rising from the earth, falling back to give life) and compares itself to a song that returns to its creator with love.

1. **Important Points:** Rain purifies and beautifies the earth.
2. **Literary Devices:** *Personification* (The rain speaks), *Metaphor* ("Poem of Earth").
3. **Exam Questions:** *How is the rain similar to a song? (6 Marks)*
4. **Answer Writing Tips:** Explain the cycle: Both originate from the heart/earth, travel to fulfill their purpose, and return to the source with love.
