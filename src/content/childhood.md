# Childhood

📝 **Short Summary:** The poet wonders when he lost his childhood. Was it when he realized Hell and Heaven aren't real (Rationalism)? Was it when he saw adults are hypocrites (Hypocrisy)? Or when he realized his mind was his own (Individuality)? He concludes childhood hides in an infant's face.

1. **Important Points:** The transition from childhood to adulthood is marked by logic, observing hypocrisy, and gaining independent thought.
2. **Literary Devices:** *Refrain* ("When did my childhood go?"), *Antithesis* (Hell and Heaven).
3. **Exam Questions:** *What is the poet's realization about adults? (3 Marks)*
4. **Answer Writing Tips:** Use the three key concepts: *Rationalism, Hypocrisy, and Individuality*.
