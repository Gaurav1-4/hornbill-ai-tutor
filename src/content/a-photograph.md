# A Photograph

📝 **Short Summary:** The poet looks at an old photograph of her mother at the beach with two cousins. The mother used to laugh at the photo. Now, the mother has been dead for 12 years, leaving the poet in utter silence and grief.

1. **Important Points:** The sea "which appears to have changed less" highlights human mortality.
2. **Literary Devices:** *Alliteration* ("terribly transient"), *Oxymoron* ("laboured ease of loss").
3. **Exam Questions:** *What does 'terribly transient feet' mean? (2 Marks)*
4. **Answer Writing Tips:** Use the 3 stages: The mother's childhood, the mother's middle age (remembering the past), and the poet's present (grieving the mother).
